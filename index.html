Best Places to Explore Near Tampara Resort

Discover amazing attractions and natural beauty surrounding our resort, from ancient shrines to serene beaches.

Taratarini Temple

At the bank of River Rushikulya Tara Tarini Temple is a famous Hindu shrine of Adi Shakti on Purnagiri or Kumari hills near Berhampur city in Ganjam district of Odisha. Purnesvari Maa Tara Tarini on Purnagiri hills is worshiped as the Breast Shrine of Adi Shakti.

Near Berhampur City

Gopalpur Beach

Gopalpur is a famous sea beach and a tourist destination. Gopalpur is around 15 km from Berhampur. While you are at Gopalpur, be sure to experience the magical sunrise & sunsets, steal a moment of solitude & enjoy a scenic boat ride.

15 km from Berhampur

Tampara Lake

Tampara Lake is a largest fresh water lake located in Ganjam District state of Odisha. It is around 3 km distance from Chatrapur and 24 km away from Berhampur. The beautiful lake and the nearby Chilika Lagoon highlight the ecological diversity.

3 km from Chatrapur

Bhairabi Temple

Siddha Bhairavi Temple is one of the famous Shakti Pitha in Ganjam. It is situated 15 km distance from Berhampur town. 108 temples are having different gods and goddesses around the Siddha Bhairab, which is more attractive to the place.

15 km from Berhampur

Balakumari Temple

Balakumari temple is situated on the top of the Kerandimala mountain, and it is 9 km from Chikiti and 37 km distance from Brahmapur in the District of Ganjam. Maa Balakumari is presiding in this temple. Devotees believe that the mother fulfils all their desires.

37 km from Brahmapur
